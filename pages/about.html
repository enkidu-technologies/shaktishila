About

Our Company

The company SHAKTISHILA ENGINEERS & MANUFACTURERS PRIVATE LIMITED encourages people to reuse and recycle refuse materials into renewable energy. Our company also provides consulting services for the conversion of biowaste to biogas and bioenergy facilities, guiding clients through the initial stages of completing and launching their projects. In addition, we help them acquire funds and apply for government subsidy programs. At Shaktishila Engineers & Manufacturers Private Limited, we provide comprehensive solutions for biogas and bioenergy projects, focusing on converting biowaste into renewable energy. Our expertise spans from initial planning to successful implementation, ensuring sustainable and efficient energy solutions.

Our Vision

Creating a greener and cleaner future by maximizing the potential of waste materials and fostering the adoption of renewable energy solutions worldwide.

Our Mission

To transform refuse materials into renewable energy sources and promote sustainable waste management practices, while providing comprehensive consulting services to clients for bio waste-to-bio energy projects.
